
- The office manager can register Dentists to their network.
- The Office Manager can enroll new patients.
- The patient can request appointments.
- The patients should be able to sign-in to the system and view their appointments.
- The patients should also be able to request to cancel or change their appointments.
- The dentists can sign-in to the system and view a listing of all their Appointments.
- The dentists cannot have more than 5 appointments in any given week.
